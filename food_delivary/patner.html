Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about partnering with FreshBite

How long does the application process take?

Typically, the application review process takes 3-5 business days. Once approved, the onboarding process can take an additional 3-7 days depending on your restaurant's readiness and our integration requirements.

What are the commission rates?

Our commission rates vary based on the partnership plan you choose. The Starter Plan has a 15% commission, the Professional Plan has a 12% commission, and Enterprise Plans have custom pricing based on volume and requirements. There are no setup fees or hidden charges.

Do I need special equipment or software?

We provide a tablet for order management at no extra cost for our Professional and Enterprise partners. For Starter Plan partners, you can use our web-based dashboard from any device. We integrate with most POS systems, or you can use our simple order management tools.

How do I get paid?

We process payments weekly via direct deposit to your bank account. You'll receive a detailed statement showing all orders, commissions, and net payout amounts. Our payment system is secure and transparent.

Can I set my own delivery radius?

Yes, you can customize your delivery radius based on your capacity and the areas you want to serve. We recommend starting with a smaller radius and expanding as you become comfortable with the delivery process.
