Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location, restaurant preparation time, and current demand. Standard delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes, while express delivery can be as fast as 20 minutes. You can track your order in real-time through our app or website.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most urban and suburban areas within our service regions. To check if we deliver to your address, simply enter your location on our website or app. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones to serve more customers.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and in some locations, cash on delivery. All online payments are processed securely through encrypted channels to protect your information.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that time, the restaurant may have already started preparing your food. If you need to make changes after this window, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

What if there's an issue with my order?

If there's any issue with your order, please contact our customer support team immediately. We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee and will work to resolve any problems, whether it's a missing item, incorrect order, or quality issue.
