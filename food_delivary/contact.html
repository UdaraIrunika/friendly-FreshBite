Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through our website or mobile app. Once your order is confirmed and out for delivery, you'll receive a tracking link that shows your driver's location and estimated arrival time.

What should I do if there's an issue with my order?

If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our customer support team immediately at +1 (555) 123-4568 or email us at support@freshbite.com. We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee and will resolve any problems promptly.

How do I become a delivery driver for FreshBite?

We're always looking for enthusiastic delivery drivers! Please visit our Careers page or email your resume to careers@freshbite.com. We offer flexible hours, competitive pay, and a supportive work environment.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes! We offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact our catering department at catering@freshbite.com or call +1 (555) 123-4569 to discuss your event needs and get a customized quote.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can rate your experience directly in the app after your delivery is complete, or you can email us at feedback@freshbite.com. Your input helps us improve our service for everyone.
